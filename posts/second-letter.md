If you’re planning to study in Germany, the process isn’t complicated —  
but it does require clarity, patience, and a lot of documents.

This is a simple breakdown of what you actually need and how the process flows.

---

## What documents do you need?

Before applying to any university, you’ll need to prepare these:

### Academic Documents
- APS Certificate  
- IELTS Certificate  
- 10th Marks Card  
- 12th Marks Card  
- Degree Certificate  
- All Semester Marks Cards  
- Official Transcript  

### Supporting Documents
- Statement of Purpose (SOP)  
- Letter of Recommendation (HOD / professor / project guide)  
- Europass CV  
- Internship / Experience Certificates (if any)  

---

## What should you check before applying?

Every university has slightly different requirements. Always check:

- IELTS score requirement (usually 6.5 – 7.5)  
- Course-specific subject requirements  
- Application deadlines  
- Whether German language certification is needed  

---

## How do you apply?
There are two main ways:
### 1. Direct Application  
Apply through the university’s official website.
### 2. Through UniAssist  
UniAssist verifies your documents first and then forwards your application to the university.

---

## How does the process actually go?

### 1. APS Certificate
- Takes around 1.5 to 2 months  
- Apply using academic documents  
- Send printed documents and transcripts by post  

Cost: ₹18,000  

---

### 2. IELTS Exam
- Around 1 month of preparation  
- 3-hour exam  
- Results in about 1 week  

Sections:
- Reading  
- Writing  
- Listening  
- Speaking  

Typical score: 6.5 – 7.5  

Cost: ₹17,000  

---

### 3. Applying to Universities
- Apply through portals or UniAssist  
- Upload all documents  

Waiting time:
Up to 3 months  

Application fees:
15€ – 30€ per university  

---

### 4. Blocked Account & Insurance
- Required for living expenses  
- Around 992€ per month (for 1 year)  

---

### 5. Visa Process
- Apply after receiving admission  
- Includes biometrics  

Time:
Around 2 months  

Cost:
₹10,000 – ₹15,000  

---

### 6. Accommodation & Travel
- Start searching for accommodation early  
- Book flights after visa approval (safer option)  

---

## How long does everything take?

From start to finish: Around 6 to 9 months

---

## Final thought

Most of this journey is just documents, waiting, and staying patient.

There’s no single difficult step —  just a lot of small ones that need to be done right.

And then one day, you land.

And everything starts to make sense.
